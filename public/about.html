📄 About TimeNow

TimeNow is your reliable companion for navigating time across the globe—built for remote workers, international teams, travelers, digital nomads, and anyone who lives or works across time zones.

Our Mission

In a world where collaboration happens 24/7 across continents, confusion over "what time is it there?" shouldn't slow anyone down. We created TimeNow to make time simple, accurate, and beautiful. Whether you're scheduling a meeting between Wisconsin and Tokyo, planning a flight to avoid jet lag, or just checking sunrise in Paris, TimeNow delivers precise, real-time information with zero hassle.

We believe time should work for you—not the other way around.

What Makes TimeNow Different

  • Atomic-level accuracy — We sync with NTP servers so your displayed time matches reality within milliseconds. See exactly how accurate your clock is.
  • Intelligent timezone handling — Auto-detect your location, support for every IANA timezone, automatic DST adjustments, and clear explanations of offsets and changes.
  • Live world clocks & pinning — Keep an eye on multiple cities at once, reorder them, and save your favorites forever (stored securely in your browser).
  • Smart scheduling tools — From meeting overlap finders and event time adjusters to jet lag planners and relative time translators ("tomorrow morning" in your zone = what time in theirs?).
  • Sun data — See sunrise, sunset, and daylight hours for any city.
  • Beautiful & readable design — Large, clear typography, dark/light modes, responsive layout, and subtle animations that make checking time feel effortless and modern.
  • SEO-friendly city pages — Dedicated pages for thousands of cities with maps, detailed timezone info and growing AI-generated insights (history, tourism, economy, FAQs).

Who We Built This For

  • Remote teams juggling calls across 5+ time zones
  • Freelancers coordinating with clients worldwide
  • Travelers minimizing jet lag and planning itineraries
  • Families staying connected across oceans
  • Anyone tired of unreliable converters or cluttered apps

TimeNow started as a simple single-page clock to solve a personal pain point (coordinating across CST and other zones), and it grew into a full-featured global time hub because the need was real—and still is.

Our Values

  • Privacy first — We don't track you, sell data, or require accounts. Everything personal (like pinned cities) stays in your browser via localStorage.
  • Accuracy & reliability — We prioritize correct timekeeping and transparent sources over flashy features.
  • Accessibility — Large text, high contrast options, ARIA labels, and keyboard-friendly navigation.
  • Free core experience — Most features are completely free; we may add optional premium perks in the future (ad-light, advanced exports, etc.) without gating essentials.

Behind the Scenes

TimeNow is an independent project built with love using modern web technologies: Node.js on the backend for fast APIs and dynamic pages, vanilla JavaScript + CSS for a lightweight, speedy frontend, and data from trusted sources like IANA timezones and OpenStreetMap.

We're constantly improving—adding more cities, smarter AI tools (like personalized overlap suggestions), better mobile experience, and curated content for every location.

Thank you for using TimeNow. We're excited to help make your global life a little easier, one tick at a time.
